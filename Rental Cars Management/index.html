Frequently Asked Question

We prioritize the customer above all else and are committed to providing them with unwavering support.

How do I book a rental car?

You can book a car directly on our website by selecting your desired rental dates and vehicle type. After entering your information and payment details, you will receive a confirmation email with your booking details.

What do I need to rent a car?

You will need a valid driver's license held for a minimum of one year, a credit card in the driver's name for the security deposit, and, if you are a foreign visitor, your passport may be required.

What is included in the rental price?

Our rental prices typically include comprehensive insurance, road assistance, and all taxes. Additional options like GPS, child seats, or additional driver coverage are available for an extra fee.

Do you offer one-way car rentals?

Yes, we do offer one-way rentals for an additional fee. You can pick up the car in one location and drop it off in another.

What should I do if I have an accident?

In the event of an accident, contact our 24/7 emergency line immediately for assistance. Please do not leave the scene of the accident until the appropriate authorities have completed their report.
